RULES & OVERVIEW

  1. The competition is open only for all Arya Vaishya community members.
  2. There will be only three formats in the competition, i.e. Solo dance, Couple dance, duet dance. One participant can participate in more than 1 category in below combination:
    • Solo + Couple
    • Solo + Duet
  3. There are only two groups: Group A: DOB between 13July 2001 to 13 July 2010 Group B: DOB before 13July 2001.
  4. There is no separate Prize category for Couple dance or Duet dance.
  5. In case of Duet dance, age of the elder participant will be considered for the Age group (Note: you cannot change your partner in further rounds) and Prize will be given as shared prize with your partner.
  6. Registration fee is Rs. 400 for Group A(9-18 Years) and Rs. 500 for Group B (18 years +).
  7. The last date of registration is 24 June 2019.
  8. The participant needs to send their dance song on av.danceclub@gmailcom in MP3 format and also needs to bring in USB stick and mobile at the venue.
  9. Maximum 60 participants will be allowed from Each group for audition round.
  10. Total 21 contestants will be qualified from Each group for the second round (Semi-final).
  11. Total 12 contestants will be qualified from Each group for the third round (Grand finale).
  12. Participation is allowed for contestants of any city but the Venue for all three rounds will be at Pune only. Note:- Contestants from Outside Pune can claim traveling allowance at actual* for only Final round. *T & C apply Max allowances Rs 1500/- on submission of appropriate proofs. DOJ on traveling ticket to Pune should be after 3rd June 2019 and return ticket DOJ should be after 3 days of the final round.
  13. Contestants outside of Pune who cannot travel to Pune for the audition round can send YouTube video link of their dance performance on av.danceclub@gmailcom by 25th June 2019.
  14. The participant should be available at the auditorium for respective rounds on the allotted time, otherwise, the registration will be cancelled for further rounds.
  15. Voting system after Semifinal: 25% weightage will be given to the voting (but only one vote for each group is allowed from any mobile number), 75% weightage is reserve for judges decision.
  16. The voting line will be closed after 2 days of Semifinal and result of Semifinal will be disclosed on 9th July 2019.
  17. Appreciation Certificate will be awarded to each participant who appeared for the audition and Semifinal. A small memento/souvenir will be awarded to all other finalists.
  18. The Certificate collection is the own responsibility of the participant after the respective round.
  19. Max. timing for the performance: audition round-2minutes, Semifinal-3minutes, final- 4minutes.
  20. The judge's panel will be Professional dance choreographer outside our community.
  21. Judges decision will be the final decision.
  22. Result of the competition will be declared after final round dance performance on the same day. Prize distribution ceremony will be held thereafter.
  23. Snacks will be available at a food stall on chargeable basis at the venue.
  24. The committee has rights for alteration /correction in the above plan/rules due to any unavoidable reasons if any.
  25. All the above rules are binding for each participant.
  26. Introducing GOLDEN TICKET to Finale, if you give your best performance in audition then two of you from each group stand a chance to land straight to Finale. After audition of all the participants, the judges evaluate which two participants will go directly to the Finale.

      27. For any queries, kindly write to us on av.danceclub@gmail.com

      Let the dance begin!
